Disability Living Allowance (DLA) for children: DLA rates for children - GOV.UK
DLA for children is a tax-free benefit with 2 components:
Care component (weekly rates):

Lowest: £24.45
Middle: £61.85
Highest: £92.40

Mobility component (weekly rates):

Lower: £24.45
Higher: £64.50

How DLA for children is paid:

Usually paid every 4 weeks on a Tuesday
Paid before bank holidays if the payment date falls on one
Paid into your bank, building society or credit union account

Extra help:

You might qualify for Carer's Allowance if you spend at least 35 hours a week caring for a child who gets the middle or highest care rate of DLA
